Garbage Man

Skills and Qualifications

Garbage men need specific skills and qualifications to perform their duties effectively. They must have a valid driver's license and be physically fit to handle the strenuous nature of the job.

Safety training is crucial, as they must operate heavy machinery and handle potentially hazardous materials. They must adhere to safety guidelines to protect themselves and the public.

Time management skills are essential for maintaining collection schedules and ensuring that waste is collected and disposed of efficiently.

Knowledge of recycling and waste management practices is important for promoting sustainable waste disposal methods.
