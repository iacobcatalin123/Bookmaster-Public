Garbage Man

Overview

The role of a garbage man, also known as a sanitation worker, is essential for maintaining public health and environmental cleanliness. Garbage men are responsible for collecting and disposing of waste from residential, commercial, and industrial areas.

Responsibilities

A garbage man's responsibilities include driving waste collection trucks, picking up waste and recyclables, and ensuring proper disposal at designated facilities. They must follow strict safety protocols and handle hazardous materials with care.

Promoting recycling and waste segregation is a key aspect of their job. Garbage men often educate the public on the importance of separating recyclables from general waste.

Interaction with the community is also part of their role. They must be courteous and professional, providing a vital service that impacts the daily lives of citizens.
